Why Limnos?

Lemnos, "the wind-ridden one".

Lemnos (or Limnos) is an Eastern Aegean island, located between Lesvos and Thassos.Strong winds are a feature of the island, especially in August and in winter time, hence the nickname.

For ancient Greeks, the island was sacred to Hephaestus, god of blacksmiths and fire, who fell on Lemnos when his father Zeus hurled him headlong out of Olympus. The local people too care of him, and in return he taught them the art of blacksmith

My favorite tavernas on Limnos

Taverna Flomari

Located on the North side of Limnos, this seaside gem is a must. Enjoy fresh seafood, local vegetables, and outstanding Lemnian cuisine. Flomaria, a traditional Lemnian pasta made with local ingredients lends its name to this outstanding taverna.

Address:

Gomati Beach, Limnos 814 01, Greece

What I like about it

The most delicious grilled fish I've ever eaten! Stunning views overlooking the clear, turquoise water of the Aegean Sea.
